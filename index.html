About my practice

Finding steady ground, together

"I walk with you during these storms to stay grounded in what matters while moving forward in life."

My approach offers a calm, safe, and compassionate space where you can stay centered through whatever you're facing — anxiety, depression, and the emotions that come with them.

I also have extensive experience supporting people through life transitions — the prenatal and postpartum period, empty nesting, becoming a caregiver, and the end of a relationship — and I'm passionate about working with people in healthcare, including physicians, nurses, and therapists.

Treatment approach

How I work

I draw from a range of evidence-based modalities and tailor the mix to what each person actually needs.

Acceptance & Commitment

Building psychological flexibility to act on your values, even alongside difficult thoughts and feelings.

Attachment-Based

Looking at how early relationship patterns shape the way you connect with others now.

Compassion Focused

Developing a kinder, steadier relationship with yourself, especially in hard moments.

Emotionally Focused

Working with the emotions underneath a conflict or pattern, not just the surface behavior.

Family & Marital

Addressing relationship and family dynamics directly, with room for more than one voice.

Mindfulness-Based

Using present-moment awareness to interrupt patterns of worry and rumination.
